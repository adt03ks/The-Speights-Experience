What to Expect

A polished, personal, and well-guided Perry Homes experience.

Buying a new Perry home should feel exciting, organized, and elevated. The Speights Experience is designed to help you explore available plans, compare lifestyles, understand your purchase path, and feel fully supported from first conversation to closing day.

Whether you are focused on a quick move-in opportunity, a homesite with the right view, or building from the ground up, Angela will help you narrow the choices and move with clarity.

  • 1
    Personal Fact-FindingWe identify your timeline, budget, must-haves, preferred rooms, homesite goals, and financing path.
  • 2
    Curated Home TourYou receive a focused tour of plans and inventory that match the way you want to live.
  • 3
    Plan + Homesite StrategyWe compare one-story and two-story plans, lot placement, exterior style, and overall value.
  • 4
    Numbers + Incentive ReviewWe review estimated payment, incentives, closing costs, lender credits, and next steps.
  • 5
    Contract to Closing SupportFrom My Account updates to walkthroughs, construction milestones, and key delivery, you are guided throughout the process.
